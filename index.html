Wei Gao

PhD Candidate at MIT

Biography

I am a PhD candidate at Department of EECS, MIT. I’m working with Prof. Russ Tedrake in Robot Locmotion Group. Previously, I also worked with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka as a visiting student.

I’m generally interested in Robotics, with subfields include robot manipulation, planning and control, 3D perception. I also enjoy building large, sophisticated systems, for instance surfelwarp for 3D dynamic reconstruction.
